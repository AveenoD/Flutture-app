Problem Statement

OS Restrictions on Call Recording

Software-only solutions are unreliable due to tightening privacy controls on both major mobile platforms.

iOS Restrictions

No Public API Access

  • Sandbox prevents background audio access during active calls.

  • CallKit does not expose audio streams to third-party apps.

  • Merge call workaround (3-way calling) is complex and carrier-dependent.

Android Limitations

API Blocked Since Android 9

  • Accessibility Service workaround often blocked by Google Play policy.

  • Microphone mute during calls prevents capturing remote audio.

  • Inconsistent behavior across OEMs (Samsung vs Pixel vs Xiaomi).
